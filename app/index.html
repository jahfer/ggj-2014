↓  How to play  ↓

Players
Move them around with the WASD keys

Prisms
Race to the prism to get points +10, and turn into a ghost.

Ghosts
Can only be seen on your screen. Hunt players for points +10 pts or race to the next prism to stay a ghost +10 pts

Obstalces
Don't run into these.

Powerups
Race to powerups to increase speed, grab a shield, or turn invisible. Avoid the red ones!

This is what your player will look like once you've picked one up.
